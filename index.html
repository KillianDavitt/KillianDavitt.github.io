    I am a Postdoctoral Research Assistant at King's College London. I work with Dr. Kovila Coopamootoo as part of the AGENCY project.

    Previously, I was a PhD student in the information security group at University College London (UCL). My PhD is titled "Improving the usability and security of low and high-latency anonymity networks". I am supervised by Prof. Steven Murdoch

    Part of my PhD work related to web browser security. In particular I investigated HTTPS Only Modes, as well as methods of using HSTS effectively in Tor Browser.
